# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in this project a respectful and
harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent while staying precise about problems.
- Keep discussions focused on the project and its users.
- Do not post secrets, private infrastructure details, or personal information.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or discriminatory language.
- Personal attacks or sustained disruption.
- Publishing private information without explicit permission.
- Sharing credentials, access tokens, or sensitive server details in public
  project spaces.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments, issues, pull requests, or other
contributions that do not follow this code. Repeated or severe violations may
lead to temporary or permanent bans from project spaces.

Report conduct concerns privately through the maintainer contact listed in
`SECURITY.md` or the repository owner's GitHub profile.
